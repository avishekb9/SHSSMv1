About the School

A center of excellence providing value-based, interdisciplinary education.

The School of Humanities, Social Sciences and Management (SHSSM) has been established to provide value-based education to the budding technocrats of the institute. The School is actively engaged in teaching and research in the core fields of Economics, English, Psychology, and Philosophy.

Besides offering courses to B.Tech., M.Tech., M.Sc., and Ph.D. students, the School is deeply integrated with the academic and extra-curricular life of the institute, aiming to produce professionals who are not only competent in their fields but also sensitive to the needs of society.

Our Vision

"To be a centre of excellence in the field of humanities, social sciences and management, and to produce professionals sensitive to the needs of the society."

Core Research Areas

A curated exploration of interdisciplinary inquiry designed to address contemporary challenges.

Economics

Applied Microeconomics, Development Economics, Environmental Economics, Financial Economics, and Health Economics.

English

English & American Literature, Indian Writing in English, Post-Colonial Literature, and Linguistics.

Psychology

Cognitive Psychology, Health Psychology, Industrial & Organizational Psychology, and Social Psychology.

Philosophy

Philosophy of Mind, Philosophy of Language, and Analytic Metaphysics.

Advanced Laboratories

Humanities & Social Sciences Lab

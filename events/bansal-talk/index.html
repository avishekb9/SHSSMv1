Guest Lecture

Harmony Amidst Discord

Navigating Conflicts in Student Life and Beyond

School of HSSM, IIT Bhubaneswar Interactive Session with Q&A
SKB

Mr. Sunil Kumar Bansal, IPS (Retd.)

Former Director General of Police, Odisha

A senior advisor and mentor with unmatched governance and administrative experience. Starting as an Indian Police Service (IPS) officer of the 1987 batch, he held several senior positions in the Government of India, finally retiring as the Director General of Police, Odisha, in December 2023.

In his two-year stint as DGP Odisha, he led a 70,000-strong force. He revamped the Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) and led relief operations after the Balasore train tragedy in June 2023. He spent substantial years with the Intelligence Bureau, excelling in information analysis, policy formulation and strategy.

Mr. Bansal served in the Indian High Commission in Ottawa, Canada (2008–12) under the Ministry of External Affairs, and was located in Mumbai (2017–21) covering economic intelligence, financial robustness and cyber fraud oversight.

About the Talk

“Time-tested techniques can be mastered by anyone to handle conflicts with aplomb, ensuring mental peace, productivity, and minimal friction in interpersonal relations.”

Conflicts are an inevitable part of life at every level. During this interactive session, Mr. Bansal will elaborate the dynamics of conflict management and techniques of promoting harmony in life, drawing from his decades of high-stakes crisis management in governance, intelligence and strategic security.

Topics Covered

Conflict Resolution
Governance & Leadership
Strategic Decision-Making
Diplomacy & International Relations
Interpersonal Harmony

About the Conference

A three-day international gathering of researchers, regulators and industry practitioners exploring the intersection of finance management, derivatives, ESG stocks and green bonds — through keynotes, panel discussions and interactive workshops.

A distinguished organising and scientific committee of faculty and researchers from IITs, IIMs, central universities and policy institutes curates the conference programme.

Conference Themes

Research tracks exploring sustainability at the intersection of finance, policy and innovation.

Core Sustainable Finance

Green bonds, ESG integration, climate risk modelling, sustainable investment frameworks and impact measurement.

Circular Economy & Entrepreneurship

Business models for circularity, waste-to-value chains, green entrepreneurship and sustainable supply chain finance.

ESG & Green Bonds

Environmental, Social and Governance frameworks, green bond markets, carbon pricing and sustainability reporting standards.

Climate-Aligned Economics

Climate finance policy, carbon markets, just transition economics and Paris Agreement alignment strategies.
